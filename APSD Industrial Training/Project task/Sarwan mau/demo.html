Meet new and interesting dogs nearby.

Tindog is a mobile app that connects dogs and their owners with other dogs and their owners in their area.

Download

Easy to use.

Simply create a profile for your dog and start swiping through other dogs in your area.

Matches.

When you and another dog owner both swipe right on each other's profiles, it's a match! You can then message each other and set up a playdate.

Location-based.

Tindog uses your location to show you other dogs and their owners in your area.
